Fruit Smoothie

Description

This Basic Fruit Smoothie is a refreshing blend of strawberries, banana, and peaches, combined with fruit juice and ice for a cool, satisfying treat. Perfect for breakfast, a midday snack, or a post-workout boost, this smoothie is both delicious and easy to make. Customize it by experimenting with your favorite fruits and juices to create your own unique flavor combinations.

Ingredients

  • 1 quart of strawberries, hulled
  • 2 fresh peaches, peeled/pitted and sliced
  • 1 banana, broken into chunks
  • 2 cups of ice
  • 1 cup of orange-peach-mango juice

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients, then combine strawberries, peaches, and a banana into blender; blend until smooth.
  2. Add ice and pour in juice; blend again to desired consistency.
  3. Serve and enjoy.
