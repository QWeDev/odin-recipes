Apple Pie Recipe

Description

This beloved apple pie recipe, inspired by Grandma Ople's legendary creation, is the perfect blend of sweet, tender apples and a rich, buttery caramel sauce—all wrapped in a flaky, golden crust. Each bite brings the comforting taste of tradition, making it a must-have for holidays, family gatherings, or cozy evenings at home. Simple ingredients, nostalgic flavor, and guaranteed to impress—just like Grandma used to make.

Ingredients

  • 8 small Granny Smith apples
  • 1/2 cup of unsalted butter
  • 3 tablespoons of all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup of white sugar
  • 1/2 cup of packed brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup of water
  • A 9 in. double-crust pie pastry

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees and Gather all ingredients. Peel and core apples, then thinly slice and set aside.
  2. Melt butter in saucepan over medium heat. Add flour and stir, then cook for 1 - 2 minutes. Add both sugars and water, then bring to boil. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 3 - 5 minutes. Remove from heat.
  3. Press one pastry into bottom and up the sides of a 9 in. pie pan. Roll out remaining pastry so it will overhang by 0.5 in.. Cut pastry into eight 1-in. strips.
  4. Place sliced apples into bottom of crust. Lay four pastry strips vertically and evenly spaced over apples; use longer strips in center and shorter strips at edges.
  5. Make a lattice cruste. Fold the first and third strips back. Lay one unused strip perpendicular over second and fourth strip, then unfold first and third strip to original position. Fold the second and fourth vertical strips back. Lay one of the three unused strips perpendicularly over top. Unfold second and fourth strips back. Repeat these steps to weave last two strips, then fold and trim excess. Pinch to secure.
  6. Slowly and gently pour sugar-butter mixture over lattice crust; let it seep over sliced apples. Brush some onto lattice.
  7. Bake in over for 15 minutes. Reduce temperature to 350 degrees and continue baking for 35 - 45 minutes or until apples are soft.
  8. Serve and enjoy.
