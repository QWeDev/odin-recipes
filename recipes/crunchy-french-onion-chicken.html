Crunchy French Onion Chicken

Description

This Crunchy French Onion Chicken recipe is a delightful twist on classic comfort food. Juicy boneless chicken breasts are coated in a crispy layer of crushed French-fried onions, creating a savory crust that locks in flavor and moisture. With just a few simple ingredients, this dish delivers a satisfying crunch and rich onion flavor in every bite. Perfect for busy weeknights or casual gatherings, it pairs wonderfully with a variety of side dishes, making it a versatile addition to your dinner rotation.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/3 cups of french-fried onions, crushed
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 lb skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees and gather all ingredients.
  2. Spread crushed onions in a shallow bowl. Pour egg into a separate shallow bowl.
  3. Dip chicken into beaten egg, then press into crushed onions. Gently tap chicken to let loose pieces fall away. Place breaded chicken on a baking sheet.
  4. Bake chicken for 20 minutes, or until no longer pink in center and juices run clear. Inser thermometer into center and confirm it's at least 165 degrees.
  5. Serve and enjoy.
